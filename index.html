Data & Privacy: How ClipMaster Uses Google Drive

We believe in total transparency regarding your data. ClipMaster includes a synchronization feature that allows you to access your clipboard history across multiple devices. To facilitate this, the app requests access to your Google Drive.

Exactly what we request and why:

  • Scope Requested: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.appdata
  • Purpose: This permission only grants ClipMaster access to a special, hidden "App Data" folder inside your Google Drive.
  • What is stored: The app creates and manages a single configuration file (clipmaster_sync.json) containing your copied text, image URLs, and pin states.

Our commitment to your privacy:

ClipMaster cannot see, read, modify, or delete your personal files, photos, or documents stored in Google Drive. Your clipboard data travels securely between your local browser and your private Google Drive. We do not have servers, and your data is never sent to us or any third parties.
