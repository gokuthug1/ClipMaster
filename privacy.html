Privacy Policy for ClipMaster

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

1. Data Storage and Location

ClipMaster is designed with your privacy as its top priority.

  • Local Storage (Default): By default, all data created and used by this extension, including your clipboard history, settings, and preferences, is stored securely on your local computer using the chrome.storage.local API.
  • Google Drive Sync (Optional): If you voluntarily choose to enable the "Cloud Sync" feature, your clipboard history will be securely synchronized to your own personal Google Drive (appData folder).

2. Data Collection and Transmission

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, clipboard history, or usage analytics to the developer or any third-party servers.

  • No Developer Access: The developer of ClipMaster has no access to your data.
  • No Analytics: The extension does not contain any tracking functionality or analytics scripts.
  • Transmission: Data is only transmitted off your device if you enable Google Drive Sync. In this case, data is transmitted directly between your browser and Google's official API servers. It never passes through any intermediary servers.

3. Google Drive Sync & OAuth Data Usage

ClipMaster offers an optional feature to back up and sync your clipboard history using your Google account. To facilitate this, the extension uses Google OAuth2.

  • Access: We request access to the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.appdata scope. This allows ClipMaster to access a specific, hidden folder in your Google Drive used solely for this application. We cannot see, read, or modify any other files in your Google Drive.
  • Usage: Authentication tokens and Drive access are used strictly to push (upload) and pull (download) your clipmaster_sync.json backup file.
  • Limited Use Compliance: ClipMaster's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Permissions

ClipMaster requests a minimal set of permissions required for its core functionality:

  • storage: To save settings and clipboard data locally.
  • clipboardRead: To detect and save copied text/images.
  • identity: To authenticate with Google Drive (only if Sync is used).
  • offscreen / scripting: To manage the clipboard and UI seamlessly in the background.

These permissions are used solely for the purposes described in the Chrome Web Store listing and are never used to track you.
